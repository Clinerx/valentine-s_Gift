In the midst of the quiet, I understood—your beauty wasn't just in your smile or the way the light caught your hair, but in the way you let me in, despite your fears. And as we sat there, hands still intertwined, I knew this would be a memory we’d carry with us—a beautiful, fragile start to something that felt as soft and delicate as the moment we first held hands.
